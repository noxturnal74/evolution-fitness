About The Gym

Malang's Premier Fitness Hub

Conveniently situated at Level 1 Hotel Helios, Jl. Pattimura No. 37, Klojen, Evolution Fitness provides a complete training environment designed for all fitness levels.

Whether you are aiming for body recomposition, functional strength, or joining high-energy studio classes, we offer quality equipment and professional guidance for safe, structured, and effective results.

  • Free Registration with Zero Joining Fees
  • Flexible Student and Public Memberships
  • Accessible Central Location with Parking Space
  • Studio Classes, Strength Area, and Personal Training
